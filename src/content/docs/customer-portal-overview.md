# Customer portal overview

Your account gives you one place to keep track of your work with Grime Time.

## What you can do here

- Review your next recommended step
- Open support and service-prep documentation
- Jump to scheduling or quote request flows
- Keep your contact information current

## What this portal does not do yet

- No online payments
- No customer-visible quote PDF history
- No automated service timeline tracking

If you need something that is not in the portal yet, use the contact page or call the team directly.
