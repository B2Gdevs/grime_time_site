# Billing and support

This section explains the basic support flow for customer accounts.

## Quotes and approvals

Quotes are still reviewed by the Grime Time team before work is finalized. If you need a formal revision, reply to the quote email or contact support.

## Day-of-service support

If something changes on the property, let the team know before the appointment window starts.

## After-service follow-up

If you have a concern after the job is complete, include:

- Your name
- Property address
- Best callback number
- A short description of the issue
- Photos if possible

That gives the team enough context to respond without extra back-and-forth.
