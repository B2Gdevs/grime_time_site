# Preparing for service

Here is the fastest way to make service day go smoothly.

## Before the crew arrives

- Make sure exterior water access is available
- Close windows and doors completely
- Move fragile decor, mats, and lightweight patio items
- Let us know about fresh paint, loose siding, or electrical concerns
- Secure pets indoors

## If you need to reschedule

Use the scheduling page or contact the team as soon as possible. Weather and access conditions can change the work window.

## Questions we may ask

- Property size or square footage
- Surface types that need cleaning
- Access issues like gates, steep lots, or heavy landscaping
- Photos if you want a faster estimate
